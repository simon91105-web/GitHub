Best Hotspots in New Flic en Flac

1. Flic en Flac Public Beach

The crown jewel of the western coast. With miles of powdery white sand and Casuarina trees providing natural shade, it's the perfect spot for weekend picnics, sunset watching, and safe swimming in the lagoon protected by coral reefs.

2. Rempart Mountain Viewpoint

Looking for an adventure? The trails leading up towards the iconic 'Matterhorn of Mauritius' offer breathtaking panoramic views of the entire Flic en Flac bay. A must-visit and highly photogenic spot for nature lovers.

3. The Coastal Road Strip

Experience the vibrant nightlife and street food culture. From authentic Mauritian 'Dholl Puri' and fresh coconut water to lively beach bars and clubs playing Sega music, the coastal road is where the town truly comes alive after dark.
